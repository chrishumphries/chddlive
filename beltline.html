Beltline Church of Christ Youth Group Mission Trip 2012 Documentary

This past summer, I was fortunate to accompany the Beltline Church of Christ Youth Group on their 2012 Summer Mission Trip to Houston, Texas. I was tasked with shooting a short documentary about their trip, as well as shoot daily "recap" videos of each day. It was wonderful to see the group in action each day as they worked to help and encourage everyone they came in contact with. This was my first "portrait" type documentary, and I learned so much working to tell the story of the group and their experience in Houston.
