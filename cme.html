UAB Computer Music Ensemble Video

I am fortunate to be a member of the University of Alabama at Birmingham's Computer Music Ensemble. Each year, we host a concert on campus, where we perform a themed concert. This year our theme was Illusions. We all composed music and visuals around the idea of what an illusion was and how we as humans interact with illusions. To begin the show, we decided to start with an illusion. We would start the show on the roof of the building our concert was in, and then halfway through, we would come into the building and finish the song live.

I was tasked with shooting and editing the video. Using two Canon t3i cameras, a shoulder mount, and a small crane, I shot the CME on the roof as well as our journey into the building. Kevin Peek, the lead singer in the video, composed the song and provided me with the music to pair the video to. It was a great video to shoot and edit, and the audience loved it live!
